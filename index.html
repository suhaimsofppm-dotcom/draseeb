About Lapo Election

Lapo Election, based in Pune, India, pioneers tech-driven campaign strategies. We specialize in connecting candidates with voters through innovative tools and analytics, ensuring impactful outreach across rural and urban areas.

Our solutions, trusted in multi-state campaigns, include Health ATMs, AI-powered videos, and real-time voter analytics, all backed by expert leadership and scalable technology.
